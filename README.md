### Hardware Installation Guide

The trailer used to carry the coffee cup is a 3D printed model that uses the dimensions shown in the above image under the Coffee Transport Solution section. This 3D printed trailer contains 4 arms, which have in total 6 locations at which screws can be used to attach the trailer to the homer base. Each of these arms connect to the coffee cup holder, which is comprised of a 70 mm circle that the coffee cup can fit into.

To install the coffee trailer, simply line up the holes in the arms with the holes on the homer base, and insert screws into the holes to attach the trailer. The holes in the trailer can fit screws up to the M3 size, which is recommended for a sturdier fit. When attaching the trailer with screws, not every attachment hole is required to be screwed in for the trailer to function. It is recommended to attach the trailer at the holes for the two long arms, as well as at least one screw for each short arm, but you could even get by with a single screw across both short arms as well.
